Welcome to Mr. Rice's Java Jukebox! When I starting building this in 2008, it was a super-big deal to find Java applets online that served as fun ways for kids to practice the basic skills of mathematics. I gathered and organized links to a bunch of them here so that kids would be able to find and use them easily.

     Of course by now, many people have moved on to downloading apps to their phones ... so I translated it all to reflect not being such a dinosaur. However, I kept the Java links as well for those who don't yet have the world in the palms of their hands. (And I kept the name of the page the same because I thought it was cool.)

     So below, you'll see links to a bunch of things that will help you practice your basic skills without even knowing that you're practicing your basic skills. Everything here should be free ... if it's not, please let me know. And everything here should be awesome ... if it's not, please let me know that as well. In other words, if you know of a free app or site that does the job better than one that I have posted, please let me know!

     Finally, I'd like to remind you that by clicking into all these resources, you're leaving Rice's domain to go out into the real-live Internet. Be careful just like you would any other time you're online. Enjoy!
